✨✨ **Hello and welcome!** ✨✨

I am a Master of Engineering (M.Eng.) student in the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS), Nangyang Technological University under the supervision of [Prof. Yong Wang](http://yong-wang.org/).

## Research Interests
I am deeply interested in ***VISxAI*** — the intersection of visualization and artificial intelligence, currently exploring how visualization can help users better understand Large Language Models' **Reasoning processes** and foster **Human–AI alignment**.
